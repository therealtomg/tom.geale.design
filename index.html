Design Impact Statement

I’m an empathetic, curious and imaginative interaction designer who aims to create meaningful and enriching designs that people love. I have an underlying belief that all people are owed helpful, life-improving experiences regardless of ability or knowledge. This desire leads me to prioritise empathy in everything I do, using it as my compass to guide me through the design process. I am committed to life-long learning, seeking knowledge from designers in my field and the broader community for whom I wish my designs to inspire.

Spending formative years of my career in the IT industry, I have developed a strong technical skill set which enables me to take my designs beyond prototypes and quickly turn them into working, functional applications that have real world impact. In addition to my technical skill set, the lessons I’ve learnt over my three years as a design student have equipped me with the theoretical knowledge I need to address nuanced design problems and create solutions which meet expectations.

About me

I'm a UX Design graduate and experienced technology professional located in Brisbane.

I started my career in IT and developed a passion for creating experiences that bring people joy.

Throughout my university degree and IT career, I've used human-centred design principles and user experience research methods to deliver high fidelity software designs, iterate and test engaging customer experiences and meet the needs and desires of stakeholders.
